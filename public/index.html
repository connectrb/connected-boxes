Post#1: 25 Years Of Drawing Boxes

My two daughters have distilled down my entire career down to a single sentence: “Dad gets paid to talk and draw boxes.” They aren’t wrong. I have been very fortunate to live in the space between a problem and a solution. Usually that space is filled with a lot of coffees, frantic deadlines and yes, many boxes. I have realised that my daughter’s summation is actually the most grounded way to describe what I do. Its all about solving problems - - What in is the box? - What stays out of the box? - And how do we draw lines so that everything actually talks to each other? ...
